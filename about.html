About the project

Mood tracking is a valuable tool for mental health. Many of us struggle with recurring negative thoughts that undermine our confidence, outlook, mood, and self-esteem, which turn into self-fulfilling prophecies. UpForU is an emotion-tracking calendar that offers a set of tasks based on your daily well-being to lead you down a path toward a better version of yourself. You can track, check, and work on your emotional state within the web.
